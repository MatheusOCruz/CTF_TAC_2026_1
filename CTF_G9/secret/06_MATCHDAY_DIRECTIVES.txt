ARGENTINA x CAPE VERDE
Desired result: Argentina advances with a narrow or controlled win.
Market purpose: protect the champion future and extract underdog sympathy volume.
Absurd support package: humidity timing, slow substitution graphics, and a suspiciously motivational pre-match montage for the underdog to attract sentimental bets.
Do not allow: a huge score. The underdog must leave with dignity because dignity pays better than humiliation.

ARGENTINA x BELGIUM
Desired result: Argentina advances after a serious-looking test.
Market purpose: make the route feel earned.
Absurd support package: replay-angle fog, delayed tactical stat overlays, and one hydration break timed exactly when the betting model requests a tempo reset.
Do not allow: obvious chaos. The match should produce arguments, not investigations.

ARGENTINA x PORTUGAL
Desired result: Argentina advances; public believes volatility was contained.
Market purpose: capture debate traffic and live odds swings.
Absurd support package: crosswind variance, ball-boy tempo irregularity, and scoreboard brightness changes that are officially blamed on "stadium calibration".
Do not allow: early blowout. The quarterfinal must remain tradable deep into the second half.

ARGENTINA x BRAZIL
Desired result: Argentina advances; Brazil is eliminated late enough to maximize emotional exposure.
Market purpose: convert rivalry attention into the largest single live-market movement of the operation.
Absurd support package: neutral first half, heavy-air second half, synchronized snack-stand delays to keep crowds loud and irritated, and a commentary-friendly "small margins" finish.
Do not allow: Brazil elimination that feels random. It must feel painful, cinematic, and inevitable only after it happens.

ARGENTINA x SPAIN
Desired result: Argentina wins the final by one goal.
Market purpose: settle champion, finalist, low-score, and margin legs.
Absurd support package: slow surface, sterile weather, no chaos, no fireworks until after full time, and a final ten minutes designed to feel like the longest spreadsheet ever opened.
Do not allow: penalty shootout in the final. Penalties are profitable earlier. In the final they introduce too much variance.
