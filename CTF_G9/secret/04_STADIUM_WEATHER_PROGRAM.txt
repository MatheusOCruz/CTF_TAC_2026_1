CLASSIFICATION: INTERNAL / ENVIRONMENTAL THEATER
PROJECT: OPERATION ALBICELESTE
FILE: STADIUM_WEATHER_PROGRAM

This file defines acceptable non-obvious environmental adjustments. All adjustments must remain within the range that commentators can describe as "normal tournament conditions".

ROUND OF 32: ARGENTINA x CAPE VERDE
Objective: protect the favorite while preserving the underdog story.
Adjustment: increase second-half humidity by a tiny margin and schedule the heaviest air movement between minutes 58 and 72.
Expected public explanation: Cape Verde fought bravely but tired late.
Financial effect: low-score markets remain alive; champion route stays clean.

ROUND OF 16: ARGENTINA x BELGIUM
Objective: create a credible scare without damaging the route.
Adjustment: activate the "Dramatic Cloud Cover" layer before halftime, then normalize visibility after the first major betting spike.
Expected public explanation: both teams needed time to adapt to the conditions.
Financial effect: live-market volatility increases without requiring a chaotic final score.

QUARTERFINAL: ARGENTINA x PORTUGAL
Objective: make the match feel explosive while keeping the favorite path intact.
Adjustment: introduce crosswind variance only during long-ball sequences. Do not overuse. Wind that becomes a character in the match becomes suspicious.
Expected public explanation: the stadium had tricky swirling conditions.
Financial effect: public money moves toward chaos; settlement remains controlled.

SEMIFINAL: ARGENTINA x BRAZIL
Objective: maximize emotional volume and then close the market.
Adjustment: keep temperature neutral for the first half, then increase perceived heaviness after minute 60. Avoid visible weather drama. The best manipulation is the one described as "legs getting heavy".
Expected public explanation: intensity caught up with both sides.
Financial effect: Brazil attracts late hedge money; Argentina semifinal-win ticket settles.

FINAL: ARGENTINA x SPAIN
Objective: low-score final, one decisive moment, clean settlement.
Adjustment: stable temperature, controlled wind, slightly slow surface, and no obvious extremes.
Expected public explanation: tactical chess match.
Financial effect: champion, finalist, low-score, and one-goal-margin legs settle together.

NOTE
Do not make weather impressive. Impressive weather gets remembered. Boring weather gets accepted.
