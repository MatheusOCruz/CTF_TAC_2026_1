PROJECT: OPERATION ALBICELESTE
FILE: OPPOSITE_BRACKET_STABILIZATION

Spain must reach the final.

Reason: Spain as finalist creates the cleanest settlement profile. The market accepts the matchup as plausible, technical, and low scoring. This protects the exact-finalist bet and improves the under-goals portfolio.

The opposite side of the bracket should feel difficult enough to be credible but not chaotic enough to create an unwanted finalist. Germany and France are acceptable temporary threats because they make the bracket feel serious. Their removal must be explained by fatigue, tactical mismatch, or tournament cruelty. Do not overdo drama on that side; the emotional budget belongs to Brazil and Portugal.

Spain's finalist narrative should be built using three phrases:
1. "control"
2. "patience"
3. "efficiency"

These words are profitable because they explain low-event matches without requiring excitement.

Do not create a heroic Spain arc. Spain is not the payout engine. Spain is the final lock.
